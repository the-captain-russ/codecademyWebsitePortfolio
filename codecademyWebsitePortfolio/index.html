About Me

Born and raised in the Shenandoah Valley on the western side of Virginia, I enlisted into the US Navy right out of High School where I played keyboard for Navy Band New Orleans. As a part of the Navy Band New Orleans Express unit, I found interest in the technical side of the band: from running sound, hooking up microphones and other equipment to the inner workings of an analog synthesizer, B3 organ, and other types of keyboards

Post my Navy career of five years, I focused my interest of electronics towards formal education in computer and electronics engineering at ITT Technical Institute. While completing my associates degree with a 4.0 GPA, I finished courses outside of the electronics field including: introduction to network technology and a intro to programming course, where the instructor noticed an astuteness for the subject and suggested I switch from the electronics degree towards a software related field; however, I continued on with my electronics path and graduated from ITT in 2009 with a AAS in Computer & Electroncis Engineering.
